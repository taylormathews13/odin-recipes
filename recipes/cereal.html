Cereal

Bowl of delicious Froot Loops

Froot Loops are pretty good, but they are probably not quite a top 5 cereal. Possibly not even top 10.

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps to make Cereal

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Pour cereal into the bowl.
  3. Pour milk on top of the cereal.
  4. Enjoy with the biggest spoon you can find.
